Supported formats and limitations

Following input formats are currently supported across the whole application, and can serve as lossless sources in automated test modes:

  • FLAC - stereo, 16 bits/sample, sample rate 44.1kHz
  • WAV - stereo, 16 bits/sample, sample rate 44.1kHz, uncompressed signed linear PCM

Self-prepared tracks mode in addition to previous formats supports:

  • MP3 - stereo, sample rate 44.1kHz
    • Decoding is provided by mpg123 based (mpglib) decoder that is included in LAME v3.99.5 library.
Support for more resolutions and sample rates is planned/being looked into. However, up to version 5.0, vanilla Android offers very little with regard to audio: only 16 bits per sample resolution, and only 44.1kHz & 48kHz sample rates. Android 5.0 supposedly changes these limitations, so app will probably get upgraded in the future

Other formats/decoders are planned (ogg definitely, hoping for m4a/aac too).
